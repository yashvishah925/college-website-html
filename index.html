 ABOUT THE COLLEGE 



MITHIBAI COLLEGE is one of the most prestigious colleges in mumbai ,known for its excellent academic reputation and state of the art facilities . MITHIBAI COLLEGE OF ARTS was estabilished as a part of education initiatives taken by 
"SHREE VILLE PARLE KELVANI MANDAL "(SVKM) which is a charitable trust formed under the societies registration act and bombay public trust act in the year 1934.MITHIBAI COLLEGE was estabilished in the year 1961 as the extention of SVKM vision on its silver jubliee celebration.




 It is a college affilated to "MUMBAI UNIVERSITY" offering both undergraduate and graduate courses.

The college is accredited grade 'A' by the "NAAC".It is a popular choice among student in various courses including arts ,commerce ,science and management.It is one of the finest colleges in MUMBAI.The mission of the school is to provide its students quality education and important life skills so that they can sustain themselbves with the challenges and face the 
challenges of the future.
The institue was started with the vision"TO BE RECOGNISED AS A PREMIER EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION THAT PRACTICES  QUALITY PEDAGOGY,ENCOURAGES INNOVATION AND RESEARCHwhile instilling values and providing a vibrant environment for the holistic development of students into valuable global citizens.
